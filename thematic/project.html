    澎湖 Penhu

  • 推薦程度 ★★★★★
  • 夏日極度推薦的地點
  • 建議搭乘飛機 (不推薦乘船 = 乘坐1.8小時的雲霄飛車)
  • 跳島旅程體驗當地文化
  • 獨木舟體驗
  • 便宜又可口的海鮮


  • Recommended ★★★★★
  • Highly recommended place in summer
  • It is recommended to take a plane (not recommended to take a boat = take a 1.8-hour roller coaster)
  • Island hopping to experience local culture
  • Canoe experience
  • Cheap and delicious seafood

    • Due to the covid-19, international travel has been affected. The summer of 2020 is the period of the Penghu Fireworks Festival. During the summer vacation, we made a last-minute decision to go to Penghu for three days. We set sail two days after the typhoon. For those who do not take the boat very often, it was a disaster. The harshness of the ocean is like riding a roller coaster for 1 hour and 50 minutes. After arriving in Penghu, we visited the houses built from the coral stones and experienced the island hopping on South Penghu Marine National Park. Believe me, please bring a small bluetooth speaker so that you can have a spectacular sea and flying birds as your audience. The whole ocean is your concert. At night, the fireworks at the Penghu Fireworks Festival are breathtaking. It's hard for me to use any simple words and photos to express my inner feelings for the entire trip to Penghu. It's your time to have your own trip to experience~


    因為covid-19疫情的關係，國際間的旅行受影響，2020夏天正逢花火節的期間，我們於暑假的時候臨時決定來個三天兩夜的澎湖旅行。不常乘船的我們，在颱風天過後兩天出航，真是一場災難，海相之惡劣彷彿就乘著雲霄飛車搭乘1小時50分鐘。到達澎湖後，我們探訪了咾咕石製作的房屋、壯闊的摩西分海，並且體驗了南方四島的跳島計畫，相信我，帶上一顆小型的藍芽喇叭，可以讓你的跳島過程更加歡樂，整片海洋就是你的演唱會。澎湖花火節的煙火，令人驚艷與壯闊。最後，整趟澎湖的旅行，令我難以運用圖片與文字表達我心中的感受，就留給下一位前往的你去體會吧~

    陝西西安 Xian

  • 推薦程度 ★★★★★
  • 志工地點：西安、陝西
  • 適合探訪的城市
  • 古文物保存完整
  • 特色地方小吃：涼皮、漿水魚魚


  • Recommended ★★★★★
  • Volunteer locations: Xi'an, Shaanxi
  • A city suitable to visit
  • Ancient cultural relics are well preserved
  • Local specialty snacks

    • In July 2019, I went to Shaanxi, China to participate in volunteer activities. This was an activity that combines culture exchange and service and brought us childrens' unforgettable smiles and human history. During the short trip, we browsed the past and the present, but also saw the gap between urban and rural areas. When it comes to this journey, you cannot forget to mention local specialties. How can you skip the opportunity to taste new food when you arrive at a new place! Except that spicy, juicy, but never greasy street snack crawfish, the delicacy I was most impressed is the famous Shaanxi special cold noodle, Liangpi. Despite its simple appearance, the chewy and slightly sour taste enhances the elegance of this simple food.


    2019-7月，我前往了中國陝西從事志工活動，這是一個兼具交流與服務的行程，帶給我們的是孩子難忘的笑容與旅程中人類的歷史文化。短暫的行程中，我們瀏覽了古今，卻也見識到了城鄉差距。這趟旅程不得不說的點還有，當地的特色小吃，畢竟難得到達一個新地方，怎能跳過品嘗新美食的機會呢!其中我印象最深刻的除了那勁辣、多汁卻永遠吃不膩的街上小吃小龍蝦外，還有陝西名產涼皮，雖然外表樸素，但那Q彈的勁道與微酸的口感，無不提人味蕾。

    台北 Taipei

  • 推薦程度 ★★★★☆
  • 台灣的首都、繁華的國際化城市
  • 夜市文化、寬版划水、溜冰體驗
  • 平溪支線：侯硐、十分、平溪、菁桐 => 鐵道文化、天燈


  • Recommended ★★★★☆
  • The capital of Taiwan, a prosperous international city
  • Night market culture, wakeboard, skating experience
  • Pingxi Railway Branch Line: Houtong, Shifen, Pingxi, Jingtong => Railway Culture, Sky Lantern

    • Accompanied by my closest friends, I went to Taipei, the capital of Taiwan. I believe in this hustle and bustle city there still have many hidden attractions waiting for us to discover. Luzhou is located next to Tamsui River, with relatively calm rivers, so water sports are very popular here. We challenged ourselves with the wakeboarding. Although we were worn out and scarred after the event. The excitement shining in our eyes cannot be hidden. The railway culture of Pingxi, the lazy afternoons in HouTong, and Taipei’s urban splendor from Xiangshan are all peace and comfort we can find in this prosperous city.


    在最為熟稔的朋友的陪伴下，我前往了台北這塊台灣首都的土地，相信這座繁忙喧囂的城市仍有許多不為人知的密藏景點，等我們去發現。蘆洲位於淡水溪旁，擁有相對平靜的河川，水上活動非常盛行，在此我們挑戰了寬版划水，當然第一次嘗試的我們，活動結束後無不傷痕累累，身心疲倦，不過在眼眸中閃亮的刺激感卻是暗藏不住的。平溪的鐵道文化、侯硐與貓咪度過的悠懶下午，還有象山上觀賞到台北的都市風華，都是我們可以在繁忙的城市中找到的平靜與自在。
