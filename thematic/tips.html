    The tips to enjoy your trip

    Traveling is one of those activities that everybody wants to do more. Most people look to travel more in order to break out of their usual environment. It is a great way to relax for a bit while having fun in a new setting.


    旅行是每個人都想做的事情之一。大多數人為了擺脫自己的日常環境，而前往旅行，將自己置身於全新環境中，暫時擺脫煩惱、放鬆身心。

    Allow Yourself to Meet New People

    Traveling is not only about visiting new countries to admire their landmarks and marvel at world-famous buildings, it’s also about meeting new people from different countries. And more importantly, learning new things from one another. When you’re in a new country you have many options: Get to know locals, or fellow travelers from different backgrounds. The cliche saying “the best part of the journey is the people you meet along the way” is well-known for a reason.

    When it comes to fellow travelers, it’s easy to connect. You share a love for traveling. Plus, the unique setting you’re in will allow you to connect with the other travelers and quickly form stronger friendship. Even if they only last a short while. These encounters will provide you the possibility to have much more fun, in ways you probably hadn’t even thought of.

    If you go out of your way to befriend locals, these new people will enhance your traveling experience. Showing you the best parts of the city and hidden gems you would have never found alone. Always use caution and trust your guts but given the opportunity, locals will surely take you to great locations that the usual travelers never get to visit.


    旅行不僅是要拜訪新國家，欣賞其地標並驚嘆於世界聞名的建築，在旅行途中也可結識來自不同國家的新朋友，彼此學習新事物。當處在新地區時，可以有多種選擇：認識當地人或來自不同背景的旅行者。俗話說「旅途最重要的意義是在旅途中遇到的人」。

    認識其他旅行者的方法很簡單，你們可以分享各自旅行的趣事。並且，你當時所處的獨特環境將使您與其他旅行者有所共鳴，並迅速建立更牢固的友誼。即使也許你們只會相處一點兒時間。這些相遇將可能帶予你更多旅行上不可預期的快樂。

    如果你碰巧有與當地人成為朋友的機會，將會為你的旅行體驗增添更在地化的色彩。他們也許會向您展示城市中最具特色的景觀亦或是他們獨自的私房景點，這是你一個非當地人永遠找不到的。謹慎行事並相信自己的膽量，但如果有機會，當地人一定會帶您到通常的旅行者永遠不會去的好地方。

    Avoid Distractions

    If your journey only lasts for a short period of time, say you are only traveling as part of your holiday, you certainly want to enjoy your travels as much as possible. A great way of having more fun, and this not only applies to traveling but in general, is to block all distractions and try to be present in the moment. Yes, that means putting the phone away. Including tuning out social media.

    It’s hard to fully immerse yourself in a new environment when you’re constantly distracted by your phone and life back home. Disable all notifications, this helps you to avoid social media apps. You can even try to spend a day with your phone turned completely off if possible. This allows you to escape your regular environment and have a clearer mind while traveling. In turn, you will create memories you cherish for a long time.


    如果你的旅行十分短暫，例如只是在假期旅行，那麼你肯定希望盡可能地享受旅行。一種獲得更多樂趣的好方法，這不僅適用於旅行--阻止所有可能造成你分心或是阻止你享受旅途的乾擾。是的，這意味著暫時遠離你的手機，包括淘汰社交媒體。

    當你經常被電話和家中的生活困擾時，將很難完全沉浸在新的環境中。禁用所有通知，這可以幫助您避免使用社交媒體。如果可能的話，你可以嘗試將手機完全關閉一整天。 這樣一來，您就可以擺脫那些繁瑣的日常，並且忘卻煩惱，創建值得長期珍藏的回憶。
