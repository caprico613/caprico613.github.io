Autobiography

Hi, I’m Chris, or you can call me Yon-Gjun Hou in Chinese. I was born in Chiayi in a family of five. My brother studied in department of Optometry and had already graduated. As for me, I study in EE, Electrical Engineering, and I’m a senior right now. If I have to use one sentence to describe my personality, it would be "Never regret the consequence of what you did, but regret why you never did." This is my life motto. I’m an extroverted person in private life, so there’s no chance of getting to know me, if you don’t start a conversation. Don’t be shy and just talk to me.

I am currently collaborating with other students to work on an independent study, and the theme is "Reconfigurable Modular Robot Motion Control”. We are divided into hardware and software groups supervised by the instructor is Dr. Wei-Yu Chiu. The software team is responsible for the development of algorithms. We use genetic algorithms to select excellent genes and hybridize the excellent genes to get a better next generation. The hardware team is responsible for turning modular robots that were originally developed only on software into reality, and try to make the hardware use different sensors to learn the environment. In the hardware team, I am responsible for designing the hardware code and handling the communication problems between the software end and the hardware end.

自傳

我的名字叫侯永駿，目前就讀電機工程學系。若用一句話來形容我的性格，“永遠不要後悔你所做的一切，而後悔為什麼你從未做過。”我對不熟稔的人較為冷漠，但私底下是一個外向的人，所以想認識我的話，主動交談會是最快且便捷的一條路。

我們的專題研究是由邱偉育指導，主題是“可重構模塊化機器人運動控制”分為硬體組和軟體組。軟體組負責演算法的開發，並使用基因演算法選擇基因並對其進行雜交，造出更優秀的子代。我是硬體組的成員，而我們的團隊負責調整模塊化機器人並使傳感器了解周圍的環境。
